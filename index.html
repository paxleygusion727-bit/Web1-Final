BIOGRAPHY

The King of Curses

Ryomen Sukuna is a legendary cursed spirit known as the strongest curse in history. Feared during the golden age of jujutsu, he slaughtered countless sorcerers and became a symbol of destruction.

Reincarnation

After his death, Sukuna’s power was sealed into 20 cursed fingers. He is reincarnated when Yuji Itadori consumes one of them, allowing Sukuna to exist within his body.

Nature & Personality

Sukuna is cruel, arrogant, and sadistic. He views humans as inferior beings and takes pleasure in chaos, domination, and destruction.
